Hydrogeological Survey

We use advanced resistivity meters and geological data to pinpoint the exact location of aquifers, reducing the risk of dry boreholes.

  • Full geological report
  • Estimated depth and yield
  • Site suitability analysis
  • Permit assistance
Borehole Drilling

Using high-capacity rigs, we drill depths ranging from 40m to 400m. We handle all ground conditions with proper casing installation.

  • Residential & Commercial drilling
  • Mud rotary & DTH methods
  • Steel/PVC casing options
  • Yield testing included
Pump Installation & Solar Pumping

Supply and installation of submersible pumps, surface pumps, and complete solar hybrid systems designed to save you money.

  • Grundfos & Lorentz pumps
  • Solar panel array setup
  • Inverter & controller installation
  • Backup generator integration
Water Tank & Reticulation

Installation of pressure tanks, water storage tanks, and piping systems to distribute water efficiently.

  • Steel & plastic tanks
  • Pressure pump systems
  • Drip irrigation setup
  • Automated controls
Maintenance & Rehabilitation

Services include desilting, screen cleaning, pump repair, and water quality analysis to restore your borehole's optimal performance.

  • Borehole cleaning/flushing
  • Pump repair & replacement
  • Water quality testing
  • Yield restoration
